How to Solve the Maze


  • Read the question and choose one of the two answers.
  • Each time you select an answer, the point on the maze moves.
  • The movement path of the point changes according to your choices.
  • When you answer all the questions, your maze path is completed.


    • Notes


  • The point (user-point) moves through the maze based on your choices and the time you take.
  • Your answer time and answer tendencies are displayed on the left and right sides of the screen.
  • The choices for each question represent different tendencies, such as the imaginary world and the real world.
  • Quickly solving the maze is not necessarily the correct answer.
